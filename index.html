About Fisheye

Like a wide-angle lens, Fisheye is here to expand your vision when it comes to recruiting new professionals. Fisheye does the hard work for you; it researches and filters candidates from a pool assembled using specialized tools; it gauges candidates on criteria suitable for your company; it presents the most suitable candidates for your work environment to select; and it ensures job retention by playing the role of the negotiator and through sustained follow-ups.

Fisheye makes the hiring process efficient for your company by reducing the average time spent on assessing each candidate by X%, and by minimizing turnover rates by Y% by placing candidates that are the right fit for the job.

Business Model & Process

Fisheye works through a network of strategic partnerships with professionals both inside and outside Saudi to facilitate and enhance access to competent executives that may not have found the ideal job yet.

1. Understanding requirements
We believe that understanding customer requirements accurately and thoroughly is the first step to avoid wasting time and focusing efforts towards the right place.
2. Searching
The labor market is one of the biggest marketplaces, but getting the best one needs a professional hunter!
3. Conducting Interviews
Job interviews always consumes dozens of hours, which may be wasted with the wrong candidate. Our mission is to reduce the number of hours spent by the client by submitting only the best two candidates per job.
4. Negotiation
The process of negotiating with a potential candidate might be even more difficult than the searching process itself, so we handle this step carefully and focus on getting the best deal.
5. Following-up
We are keen on building long-lasting and effective strategic partnerships, hence our team will remain in touch during the first trial to provide any support the client or candidate may need.
