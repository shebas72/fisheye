FAQ

Can anyone be a recruiting sourcer with the Fellows?

Yes. It’s preferred to have a recruitment background, but if you think you can refer a qualified candidates, you will succeed with us

What do I get paid for?

You get paid for every ”Approved” candidate profile you submit to us for a job requirement. Candidate profiles are qualified by our Experts against the role requirements and are either 'Approved' or 'Disqualified'

Do you provide any recruitment tools?

No. We don't provide you with any Job Board or media-based recruitment tools. Since all of our recruiting sourcers are freelance professionals, you will need to have your own network and database of candidates, such as LinkedIn, Google Search or job board.

When & Where I can work?

Anytime and anywhere! Really! We have no restrictions on when or where you work. Whether you commit full time, part time, evenings or weekend, at home or in a coffee shop the choice is yours. We do recommend, however, that you do not breach any contractual relations you have with a current employer by working with us
